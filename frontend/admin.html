How It Works

When users select "bKash Send Money" as their payment method:

  1. They see your account details on the payment screen
  2. They manually send money using their bKash app
  3. They enter their transaction ID to confirm payment
  4. Admin verifies and approves the payment
Note: Ensure the account number is correct to avoid payment issues.
